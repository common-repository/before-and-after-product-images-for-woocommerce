== Installation ==

1. Goto your WordPress dashboard
2. Ensure WooCommerce is installed and activated
3. Activate Before and After Product Images for WooCommerce
4. Go to WooCommerce products via the left menu 'Products > All Products' and edit a product
5. Add your images in the 'Before and After Product Images' section
6. Your before and after product images will now appear on your product page (see tabs)
7. Customize the appearance in the settings section (WP Dashboard > Left Menu > Before and After Product Images)

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where do my before and after images appear? =

They will appear on the product page under, see tabs.

= What dimensions should I upload my images? =

We recommend uploading your images at the largest size possible with a low file size, ensure both images are the same dimensions for best results.

= Can I customize the appearance? =

Yes, see 'WP Dashboard > Left Menu > Before and After Product Images'.

== What before and after slider does this use? ==

It uses the excellent <a href="https://zurb.com/playground/twentytwenty" target="_blank">Zurb TwentyTwenty</a> script.
